Due to the perishable nature of our products, all sales are final. However, if you receive any damaged or spoiled fruit, kindly return it to the stall within 1 hour of purchase with your receipt for an immediate replacement.

  • Damaged or spoiled products must be reported within 1 hour of purchase.
  • Bring your original receipt as proof of purchase.
  • Replacements are subject to product availability.
  • We do not accept returns for buyer's remorse or change of mind.
  • Please inspect your items upon receipt to ensure quality.
